MORE ABOUT THE PROJECT
The game was a really simplistic tower defence.

As a player, you had the option to move around the map, to fight enemies, to place, upgrade or repair towers in exchange for energy, and the goal was to survive different waves of enemies and power that the Gamemaster sends upon the players.

As a Gamemaster you had the power of a god, you could place walls, send meteorites on players and create portals from where bloodthirsty monsters would spawn. And your goal was to eliminate every player on the board.
