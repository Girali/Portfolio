This project is a dev challenge, the goal was to reproduce one of my older projects "Space Velocity" in lest than 1 day, on the Godot Engine and coded with Python.

After 1 day of work the result was pretty interesting, Godot was a nice discovery, even if I did not reuse it since then.
And the game have a nice feeling even if it's not in 3D like the original but for the sake of saving some time on the visual aspects I have done it in 2D.
Like in the original the game start, and we need to avoid obstacles that come towards us, we gain points through time. Sometimes a green bonus can appear on the way and can be taken in order to gain more points.
The speed of the game is based on the amount of points we have.
The goal like in an old school arcade game is to gain the biggest score possible than the score in shared on an online scoreboard.
