Space Velocity
Space Velocity is an arcade game, where the player controls a spaceship, the goal is to collect the highest amount of points in one run. The more points you collect, the higher your speed is and to spice things up there are obstacles to avoid.

It was the first time is used Unity in my life for a project. The game has a lowpoly visual style with high contrast to stylize it more.
It was the first time that I worked on the entire graphic aspect of the game. Which helped me develop my personal visual style. The progression of this style can be seen in my latest game, Vandalhalla.
