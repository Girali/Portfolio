Darkest Room
Darkest Room is an escape game, with a PS1 visual style. The game goal is to escape the room. However, there is a limitation on the time that the player have to escape the room.
3 weeks
3 persons
Dec 2018
I worked on it as a unity integrator (I had to assemble the different parts created by all the project members).
It was a scholar project which purpose was to learn object-oriented programming in Unity. The player had to resolve a series of different puzzles, in order to escape the room. With time, the player screen get more and more blurry and dark. To prevent that, the player could use syringes hidden around the room to reduce this effect.
