Boltlorant series
Boltlorant is a series of tutorial videos that I made with the help of a friend about the creation of a networked video game based on the game Valorant from Riot Games.
6 months
1 person
Fev 2021
The videos are uploaded to YouTube and are separated in different episodes, each one representing a feature of Valorant.
The networking SDK used in the tutorial is named Bolt, and is made by Exit Games.

Exit Games references the tutorial on their website and was referencing it on their Discord for new users that needed help with the networking engine.
