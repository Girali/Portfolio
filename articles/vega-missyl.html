Super Vega Missyl Racing
Super Vega Missyl Racing is an arcade racing game based on a true story of a legend, “Étienne le Bolideur”.
The game was created to honour his passion for racing cars. And that why the game is centred around his lore and persona. The cars that the player unlocks first is the iconic Vega Missyl the signature car of “Étienne”.
5 months
3 persons
May 2018
The game is heavily inspired by Trackmania and by the universe of “Étienne”.
We wanted for the project to have lowpoly visual because it fits more the mindset and tails of “Étienne le Bolideur”. And it helped with the arcade feeling of the game.
In addition, the game's purpose and multiplayer mode, and different cars to race with which offer diverse driving experience.
This project initiated me to procedural generation, in fact the roads that can be seen on the video are procedurally generated. I wanted for the level designer to have an easy tool to use, which helped with the level designing process. (more about it here)
