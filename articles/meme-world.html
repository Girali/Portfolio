MEME WORLD
MEME World is an arcade runner, with retro neon/futuristic style. The player goal is to finish each run with to avoid falling out of the screen.
2 weeks
2 persons
Jun 2018
I worked as the developer and the other member done the graphics.
This game was created for a scholar project, it's a simple runner where you incarnate a stick man which want to escape the MEME World, but memes are here to prevent you from escaping.
The player can run, sprint, slide and jump to reach the end of the procedural generated levels. The game has a scoreboard on which all players have their best time registered.
In fact, not like in standard runner, the player can fail to jump or can even be stuck on an obstacle. What matters it the speed at what the player goes. If he is too slow, he gets cached by David Lopez (one of the memes that don't want you to escape). If he is fast, the player can beat the other players score.
After every timed level, there is a boss level, which can be achieved by avoiding the enemy attacks and throwing at them so frying pans that can occasionally spawn.
