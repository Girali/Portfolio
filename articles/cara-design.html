Character design
During my time at master degree in Art, I crafted two distinct character designs that each embody their own theme.
The first character is a woman with long prosthetic legs and a flowing robe, emphasizing a post-apocalyptic setting where survival is crucial.
Her prosthetic legs tell a story of resilience and determination, while her striking appearance hints at the harsh realities of a world in ruins.

In contrast, my second character design is a mage who harnesses the power of magic and can fly using long, flowing ribbons. She is designed for a fantasy world.
The character's elegant and graceful appearance is a reflection of her mastery over magic, allowing her to soar above the magical realm with ease.
The flowing ribbons not only enhance the character's beauty but also signify her deep connection to the elements of the magical world.
