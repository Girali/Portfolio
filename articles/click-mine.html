CLICK N’ MINE
Click n' Mine is a Cookie Clicker style game. In Click n' Mine you incarnate a worker from the Red Army, your goal is to have the biggest income possible.
It's my first game made for a school project. The project taught me basics of object-oriented programming. I used Python, PyGame and MySQL to create this game.

At first, you need to mine some ore in the mines to get money, then you can get some help from your family or other members of your village, you can even upgrade your tools or the mined ore to get more income.
This game was realized by myself, the graphic part was just some hand drawing and some scrapping of Minecraft textures.
